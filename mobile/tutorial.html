Checkers Tutorial

How to Play

  1. Objective: Capture all opponent's pieces or block them from moving.
  2. Setup: 8x8 board, 12 pieces each on dark squares of first 3 rows.
  3. Movement: Diagonally forward to adjacent empty square.
  4. Capture: Jump over opponent's piece to empty square behind it.
  5. King: Reach opponent's back row to crown piece. Kings move forward and backward.
  6. Winning: Capture all pieces or block all moves of opponent.
